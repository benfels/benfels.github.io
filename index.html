SXSE Wrapped

I took mock internships during the end of my junior year and the "company" we were "working" for was basically just South by Southwest but in Richmond, Virginia.

The idea is that each team would develop a "SXSE Wrapped" app in collaboration with Spotify. Each team ended up interpreting this slightly differently.

The mock internship period was split up into weekly projects called "sprints". The entire class was also split up into groups of ~6 members. Each member of each team could choose 1-2 out of 4 roles per sprint: Data, Design, Development, and Marketing. Each team presented to the whole class during sprints 1 and 3.

Sprint 1

For this sprint, I took the role of development and design. I was under the impression that I would keep the roles for all 3 sprints, but our instructors wanted us to tackle a different role for each sprint.

I waited until I had a good vision from the rest of the team before coding the app, and ended up finishing it within a couple hours.

Although the future development teams made their apps in Code.org, I coded the app for this sprint in raw HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

Link to the app

Sprint 2ish

For this sprint, I ended up on the data team. I just kinda had to make up data for this sprint (that were related to the personas we made between sprints one and 2ish (which by the way, I had no part in the creation of them).

For some reason, my team lacked communication for this sprint and I had to make the data myself without any other input. I made 2 data graphs, one on the music genres our personas listened to often, and the other one was just a random made-up data chart on how much our personas spent at SXSE. The data graphs are just default templates in Canva. Somehow this ended up making 2 other teams include a financial chart in their app during the final sprint, probably not the case however.

Final Sprint (Sprint 3)

Marketing was the only role I had not fullfilled yet, so I ended up doing that. The goal for the marketing role in this sprint was to produce an "epic" commercial advertising South by Southeast and SXSE Wrapped. One direction that was given is that the commercial would air during the Super Bowl (wow so cool anyways), so I had to make it look interesting and professional (to an extent at least).

It took me a while to develop an idea, but I just ended up taking the song "Restless" by Creo and took some random stock footage and random videos from YouTube to make the commercial. The commerical was made in Canva. Personally, I feel like I had the best commercial out of all the teams during the final sprint.
